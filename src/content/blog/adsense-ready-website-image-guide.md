AdSense approval depends on more than images, but image quality and speed still contribute to the overall trust of a website.

## Keep pages clean and readable

Oversized, blurry, or inconsistent images can make a site feel unfinished. Clear visuals and consistent formatting improve the credibility of content pages.

## Improve page speed

Heavy images create slower pages, especially on long-form content and archive templates. Cleaner image preparation supports a faster and more reliable reading experience.

## Match images to the article intent

Each article should have visuals that support the topic rather than cluttering the page. Relevant images help the page feel more useful and polished.

## Build a content library, not just a tool page

A strong site often includes helpful supporting content such as guides, comparisons, and publishing advice. That broader library gives the project more depth.

## Final takeaway

Well-prepared images help support the kind of fast, clear, trustworthy site experience that monetized content projects should aim to deliver.
