Ecommerce stores rely on images more than most websites, which means format decisions have a direct effect on speed and conversion.

## Product photos

Photographic product images usually work best in WebP or AVIF. Both can reduce file size compared to older formats while keeping good detail.

## Thumbnails and listing images

Smaller image slots benefit from aggressive optimization. Lightweight files help category pages and search results feel faster.

## Transparent graphics

When products or badges need transparency, PNG and WebP are still strong options. Use the smallest format that preserves the visual edge quality you need.

## Zoom and detail views

High-detail zoom images need a more careful balance. Over-compression can hurt trust if texture, stitching, or fine product details become muddy.

## Final recommendation

Use WebP as a dependable default, AVIF where stronger compression matters, and PNG only where transparency or exact edge clarity makes it necessary.
